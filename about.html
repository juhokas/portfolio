Wabi-Sabi Ceramics

Discover the essence of wabi-sabi in Juho Kastemaa's ceramic creations. Each piece is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, embracing the beauty of imperfection and impermanence. Through the wabi-sabi aesthetic, Juho seeks to evoke a sense of tranquility and appreciation for the natural world.

Moon Jar Vases

Experience the timeless elegance of moon jar vases by Juho Kastemaa. Inspired by traditional Korean pottery, these vessels capture the essence of simplicity and harmony. Each moon jar reflects Juho's mastery of form and balance, creating a serene presence in any space.
