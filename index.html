Welcome to the portfolio of Juho Kastemaa, a ceramic artist specializing in throwing ceramic pieces with a wabi-sabi style.

Recent Focus

Juho has recently been focusing on creating moon jar type Korean ceramic vases. These pieces reflect Juho's dedication to traditional Korean pottery techniques and embody the beauty of imperfection.

Techniques

Juho employs wood and gas reduction firing techniques to achieve unique surface textures and colors in his ceramic pieces. His international firings around the world have enriched his understanding of diverse firing methods and clay bodies.

Portfolio

Explore Juho's portfolio to discover a range of ceramic artworks inspired by nature, simplicity, and the wabi-sabi aesthetic.
